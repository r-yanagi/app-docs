# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 18, 2026

## Introduction

RYC ("we", "our", or "us") respects the privacy of users of the "Circadian Rhythm" application ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our App.

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information

The App may automatically collect the following information:

- **Device Information**: Device type, OS version, device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: App usage frequency, feature usage, gameplay data (scores, play count, etc.)
- **Advertising Information**: Information for ad delivery through Google AdMob

### User-Provided Information

The App does not generally collect personal information directly from users. We do not require account registration or personal information input within the App.

## How We Use Information

The collected information is used for the following purposes:

1. **App Provision and Improvement**
   - Providing app functionality
   - Bug fixes and performance improvements
   - Enhancing user experience

2. **Advertising**
   - Displaying ads through Google AdMob
   - Delivering relevant advertisements

3. **Analytics**
   - Analyzing app usage
   - Measuring feature popularity

The App does not intentionally collect personal information from children under 13. If children under 13 use the App, they should do so under parental supervision.
